# Why OpenVAS Matters for SMBs

Many SMBs do not have continuous vulnerability management in place. As a result, internet-facing systems, outdated software, and insecure configurations may remain unnoticed for long periods.

OpenVAS helps reduce that visibility gap by scanning assets and producing reports that show:
- What was scanned
- Which vulnerabilities were detected
- How severe the issues are
- What should be reviewed or remediated first

This makes OpenVAS useful for SMBs that need a low-cost way to start building a repeatable security review process.
